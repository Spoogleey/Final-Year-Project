AI-Assisted Writing Tool

Whether this is your first time ever using generative AI or you're a veteran user, this quick guide will give you some tips on how to make the most out of the text and image generating that this website offers.

Text

When writing prompts for text generation, it is important that you are clear in what you are trying to generate. If you are trying to bypass writer's block, for example, ensure that you provide the tool with some content that you have already written, such as chapters or characters, to ensure that the result is catered towards your needs. If you are looking for some plot ideas, ensure you include the genre you are aiming to write about, and any other significant topics that you have in mind such as, the length, any characters that you have already thought about, or the beginning to the plot, if you are just struggling to think of an ending. It is important to note that you should specify how many ideas you want generating, to avoid being overwhelmed. Here are some prompt examples to help you get an understanding of what you are aiming for:

  • “Generate me some ideas to continue my horror novel. My last chapter ended with the main character James, discovering the body of the past homeowner in the basement. Give me 4 ideas.”
  • “Give me some plot ideas for a superhero novel. It begins with the main character Steven, finding out he has the ability to control water. Give me 3 ideas about how the book might be structured following this.”

Images

Prompting for images differs compared to prompting for text, as you need to be much more descriptive to ensure that the images generated are actually what you had in mind. When generating images for a character, make sure that you specifiy the genre and include as many important details as possible for example, any weapons that they are holding, specific clothes that they may be wearing or specific backgrounds that would be important to describe in your book. For scenaries, again ensure that you specifiy the genre that you are writing and important details that would be important to describe, like the location or specific bits of nature that might be present. You will not need to specifiy how many image generations you will need, as the tool is automatically set to generate one image. Here or some prompt ideas for a character and a scenary:

  • “Create an image of a knight holding a big sword and shield. Design him like he is in a fantasy novel.”
  • “Create and image of a magical forest containing a pond and plenty of trees.”
